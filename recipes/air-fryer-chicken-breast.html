Air Fryer Chicken Breast

Description

Air frying is the best way to cook tender chicken in less time, since there's no preheating the oven. And with my simple seasoning blend, these flavorful chicken breasts not only make an easy weeknight dinner, but they're perfect for meal prep too. This recipe results in super flavorful, tender chicken breasts with no brining!

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken breast(s)
  • Olive oil
  • Kosher Salt
  • Oregano
  • Paprika
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. In a small bowl combine the salt and spices with oil to create a paste. Coat chicken breasts with the paste.
  2. Place in the air fryer basket and cook at 370-degrees for 10 minutes. Flip the chicken and cook for an additional 4-8 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 160-degrees.
  3. Tent the chicken with tin foil and allow to rest for 5 minutes before serving.
