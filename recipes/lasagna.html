Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion And Garlic
  • Tomato products(puree, paste)
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning(salt, pepper, herbs, etc)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the Lasagna
    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat Sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat, then top with the remaining Parmesan Cheese.
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving.
